What Is Spacebars?

Spacebars is a small privately owned company that sells customly made key caps to spice up your keyboard setup. Whether you game, or work spacebars key caps can let you express yourselves on your keyboard.

Why Use Key Caps?

Custom key caps are great for long use as they're beauty will not rub away like the painted letter key caps most keyboards have. They are also a great way for you to get lovely artwork or express yourself on your keyboard.

What Can I get?

Spacebars products only include space themed key caps. However, you are allowed to order specialized design as well by contacting us.
